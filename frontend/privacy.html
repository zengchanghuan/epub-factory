Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

At FixEpub, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

  • Files: We process the EPUB and PDF files you upload solely for the purpose of conversion and translation.
  • Log Data: We collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type) for security and performance monitoring.

2. Data Retention

  • File Deletion: Your uploaded files and converted results are automatically deleted from our servers immediately after the task is completed or within 24 hours. We do not keep copies of your documents.
  • No Personal Profiles: We do not require account registration for basic usage, thus we do not store personal profiles.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Payment: We use secure payment processors (like Lemon Squeezy or PayPal) to process payments. We do not store your credit card information.
  • AI Processing: We use LLM APIs (e.g., OpenAI/DeepSeek) for translation. No personal data is sent to these providers other than the text content of your books.

4. Contact

For privacy inquiries: support@fixepub.com

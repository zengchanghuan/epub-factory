Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026 · Operator: Shenzhen Changhuan Culture & Art Co., Ltd.

At FixEpub (fixepub.com), we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

  • Files: EPUB/PDF/MOBI/AZW3 files you upload are processed only for conversion and translation.
  • Account: If you sign in, we may collect phone or OAuth identifiers for authentication.
  • Logs: IP address, browser type, and task metadata for security and reliability.
  • Payment: Processed by third parties (e.g. Alipay); we do not store card numbers.

2. Data Retention

  • Uploaded files and results are deleted after task completion or within 24 hours.
  • We do not keep long-term copies of your documents.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Payment: Alipay and other compliant processors.
  • AI: Translation via DeepSeek API; only book text needed for the task is sent.
  • Analytics: May include Baidu Tongji / Google Analytics as configured on the site.
